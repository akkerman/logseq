#+title: He whose life has a why can bear almost any how

This quote is attributed to [[id:190c5fad-f5ca-445b-9ce9-1c5e16f4ebf1][Friedrich Nietzsche]]:  /Hat man sein warum? des Lebens, so verträgt man sich fast mit jedem wie?/

[cite:@peterson_12_rules_2018] Treat yourself like someone you are responsible for helping.

[[id:2d71009c-a13f-4a9a-a548-c3ac84d38779][Viktor Frankl]] screef in [[id:07442142-9426-4186-a654-4b0204c3ac76][Man's search for meaning.]]
#+begin_quote
As we said before, any attempt to restore a man’s inner strength in the camp had first to succeed in showing him some future goal. Nietzsche’s words, “He who has a why to live for can bear with almost any how,” could be the guiding motto for all psychotherapeutic and psychohygienic efforts regarding prisoners.
