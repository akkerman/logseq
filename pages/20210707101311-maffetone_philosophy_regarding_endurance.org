#+title: Maffetone philosophy regarding endurance

Phil Maffetone's general [[id:dde81736-7aa2-4d43-bdd0-de363bd0f78b][Philosophy]] regarding endurance contains four key points:

1. Build a great aerobic base.
2. Eat well.
3. Reduce stress.
4. Improve brain function.
