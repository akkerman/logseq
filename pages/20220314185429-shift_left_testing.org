#+title: Shift Left Testing


Shift Left Testing is an approach to testing where testing is performed earlier
in the development cycle. i.e. moved left on a timeline or down in the [[id:23373aca-1ea2-45be-a995-d66f568b2b74][Testing
Pyramid]]
