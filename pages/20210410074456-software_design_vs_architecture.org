#+title: Software Design vs Architecture

- tags :: [[id:e3ba0d13-1e68-4a29-9104-21d3ec2a41de][Software Architecture]] Â· [[id:e594e54f-1b3a-4ec7-919c-7237451ac048][Software Design]]


Er is een overlap maar algemeen:

*Architectuur* gaat over de structuur, relaties tussen componenten. (high-level)
*Design* gaat over de implementatie, data structuren en algorithmes. (low_level)


#+begin_quote
For starters, I'll assert that there is no difference betwen them. /None at all./
#+end_quote
[cite:@martin_clean_architecture_2017]

De low-level details en de high-level structuur zijn delen van hetzelfde geheel.

#+begin_quote
The goal of software architecture is to minimize the human resources required
to build and maintain the required system.
#+end_quote
[cite:@martin_clean_architecture_2017]
