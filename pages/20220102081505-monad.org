#+title: Monad

Monad is an [[id:53b6b8d0-f49f-4758-9330-07eabbedefeb][Applicative]].


The ~>>~ function is a convenience operator that is used to bind a monadic computation that does not require input from the previous computation. It is defined in terms of bind ~>>=~:

#+begin_src haskell
(>>) :: m a -> m b -> m b
m >> k = m >>= (\_ -> k)

#+end_src
