#+title: associative relation

When a relation or [[id:5070516e-6dc7-4c8b-b69e-45db03c10be2][binary operation]] is associative it means that the result will not be
different when applying the operation in a different order (e.g. parentheses)

#+begin_src
1 + 2 + 3 == (1 + 2) + 3 == 1 + (2 + 3)
#+end_src
