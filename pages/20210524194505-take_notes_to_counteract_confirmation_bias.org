#+title: Take notes to counteract confirmation bias

[[id:25f24494-0032-4d8f-8dce-a938d27fc556][Note taking]] forces you to make your tinking more explicit.
If your thinking evolves, you will be able to confront yourself with different opinions overtime.

Because your thinking is more explicit, you challenge yourself, and you will be able to avoid [[id:7551debd-fe45-40a7-b2ba-a6dc72769a86][confirmation bias]].
